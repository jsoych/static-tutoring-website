About

Hello! I'm John, your private math and physics tutor. I graduated from Lakehead University with a diploma in Civil Engineering, and study Applied and Pure Mathematics at the University of Waterloo. My academic interests include analysis and differential geometry. I have been tutoring highschool, college, and university students online and in person for over 5 years. For fun I like to rock climb, play hockey, snowboard and have just recently taken up cross country skiing.
